Yuenan HOU

I am a 2nd-year PhD student at Information Engineering Department, the Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am working in the Multimedia Lab, under the supervision of professor Tang Xiaoou. I also work closely with professor Chen Change Loy.

I graduated from Nanjing University in July 2017. Prior to that, I worked with Chunxiao Liu and Zheng Ma during my internship at Sensetime Research.

My research interests include semantic segmentation, reinforcement learning and robotic manipulation.
