HOU Yuenan

I am a 2nd-year PhD student at Information Engineering Department, the Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am working in the Multimedia Lab, under the supervision of professor Tang Xiaoou. I also work closely with professor Chen Change Loy.

I graduated from Nanjing University in July 2017. Prior to that, I worked with Dr. Liu Chunxiao and Dr. Ma Zheng during my internship at Sensetime Research.

Currently, I am working in the project of knowledge distillation (intended for ICCV 2019) in image classification and lane detection. My research interests include semantic segmentation, knowledge distillation, reinforcement learning and robotic manipulation.
