HOU Yuenan (侯跃南)

I am a 3rd-year PhD candidate at the Department of Information Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am working in the Multimedia Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Chen Change Loy and Prof. Xiaoou Tang.

I received my B.E degree from Nanjing University in July 2017, supervised by Prof. Chunlin Chen and Prof. Xianzhong Zhou. After that, I worked with Dr. Chunxiao Liu and Dr. Zheng Ma during my internship at Sensetime Research.

Currently, I am working in the project of road marking segmentation and knowledge distillation. My research interests include semantic segmentation, knowledge distillation, reinforcement learning and robotic manipulation.

Publications

Inter-Region Affinity Distillation for Road Marking Segmentation
Yuenan Hou, Zheng Ma, Chunxiao Liu, Tak-Wai Hui, Chen Change Loy
IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2020
[pdf] [code]

We present a novel knowledge distillation approach, i.e., Inter-Region Affinity KD (IntRA-KD), which can transfer ‘knowledge’ on scene structure more effectively from a teacher to a student model.

Learning Lightweight Lane Detection CNNs by Self Attention Distillation
Yuenan Hou, Zheng Ma, Chunxiao Liu, Chen Change Loy
International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV), 2019
[pdf] [code]

We present a novel knowledge distillation approach, i.e., Self Attention Distillation (SAD), which allows a model to learn from itself and gains substantial improvement without any additional supervision or labels.

AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI, oral), 2019
